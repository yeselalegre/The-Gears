What We Do

The Gears is the official school publication dedicated to sharing news, stories, and creative works. We aim to inform, inspire, and give voice to the student community through meaningful writing and visuals.

News

Covers the latest events and important announcements within the school and community.

Updates

Provides timely information about campus activities, programs, and student initiatives.

Opinion

Features insights and viewpoints from students and writers on relevant issues and topics.

Feature

Tells inspiring stories that highlight people, events, and experiences in the school.

Literary

Showcases creative works such as poems, essays, and short stories from student writers.

Komiks

Delivers stories and humor through original comic art created by talented students.

Arts

Highlights visual creativity, including drawings, digital art, and other artistic expressions.

Photo & Videos

Presents photos and videos that capture moments and stories reflecting the life of the school community.

Stay Connected with The Gears

Read the latest stories, features, and updates that keep you informed and inspired as part of our campus community.
